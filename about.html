About Me

Who am I?

I was born in San Diego, but grew up in Calgary until I was about 12 when I returned to San Diego where I went to highschool and have lived ever since. My data and computer science interest didn't start until towards the end of my freshman year at UC San Diego, when I realized I had a love for data and statistics after having a look at my own data records in a few different applications I used like Spotify, Google, and a few different video games I was playing at the time. It was this discovery that eventually led me to study Data Science at UC San Diego and to discover machine learning and its wide array of applications. I even went on to revisit Spotify data when working on my capstone project about playlist recommendation. Whether it is extracting useful insights from datasets, ensuring good data flow from databases, or creating neural network architectures for predictive modeling, I love to work with data and learn from it.
